IO Summary

  • Non Blocking IO we receive the state/event changes on fds asynchronously. But the I/O operations are handled synchronously
  • With Epoll we can monitor large no of fds(I mean sockets and other stream fds on which less activity happens) for IO and do the processing in single thread.
  • This means we can keep the connection alive(ie need not close socket fds) and keep adding as many as possible.
